About

From the age of 2, Mike Sobhi has been immersed in the real estate world. Graduating from playing with “For Sale” signs to creating them, he quickly took to the industry. Wanting to expand his horizons, he pursued and earned a degree in marketing and finance. His detour ultimately gave him a richer understanding of the market and how to effectively buy and sell properties; an opportunity not everyone has. With his unique wealth of knowledge, Mike strives to ensure his clients are making informed, confident decisions.

Priding himself on developing a unique understanding of his clients’ needs, he is able to use modern and traditional methods to deliver high quality results. Harnessing cutting-edge technology and building relationships founded on honesty and trust, Mike will make the process of buying or selling your home stress-free, exciting, and simple.

Mike knows, you’re not just looking for a house, but a home.
